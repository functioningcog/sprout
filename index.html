Welcome to Sprout

Sprout is a small but spirited café tucked away in the heart of Exeter’s Cathedral Yard. We serve colourful, wholesome, plant-based meals inspired by the seasons. Our food is full of flavour, thoughtfully prepared, and open to all — whether you eat plant-based every day or just fancy something fresh.

We don’t have a set menu. Our food changes with what’s good and fresh at the time — meaning no two visits are ever quite the same. Come by and see what’s on offer today.
